1. What this website stores

The Project Caligo website does not currently place cookies on your device or use local storage, session storage, tracking pixels, advertising identifiers, or browser fingerprinting.

The site also does not currently use Vercel Web Analytics or another audience-measurement product. The contact form works without placing a cookie.

2. Why there is no consent banner

Cookie consent banners are used where a website needs permission for non-essential cookies or similar browser technologies. Since this website does not use those technologies, displaying an accept-or-reject banner would create a choice about something that is not happening.

This notice does not mean the website processes no technical information at all. Vercel, our hosting provider, necessarily receives ordinary network requests and may process IP addresses, request details, and security logs to deliver and protect the site. That server-side processing is explained in the Privacy Notice; it does not mean Project Caligo has stored a cookie in your browser.

3. External websites

If you follow a link to Discord, open an email application, or visit another external service, you leave this website. Those services may use their own cookies or similar technologies under their own policies and controls. Project Caligo does not control cookies placed by a separate website you choose to visit.

4. If this changes

If we later add analytics, embedded media, authentication, advertising, or another feature that uses cookies or similar storage, we will update this notice before or when the feature is introduced. Where applicable law requires consent, we will also add a suitable consent control before activating the non-essential technology.

5. How to verify or ask

You can inspect your browser’s developer tools or site-data settings to confirm what a website stores. Questions about this notice can be sent to contact@industrium.net.
